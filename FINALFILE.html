3DARVisualizer

Debugging 3D Models in CAD

About

CAD is gradually becoming a skill that has become more significant in STEM. As a result, students at lower education levels are beginning to interact with CAD software. 3DARVisualizer is an application that helps novice CAD users find errors in their models and develop their spatial visualization skills utilizing Augmented Reality (AR) technology. The application is currently hosted on Heroku and is compatible with any device containing a camera.

PART 1:

Download the marker on your device or print it out.


Convert the desired CAD model obtained from Tinkercad into a .stl file. Otherwise, download the CAD model database above for sample models.

PART 2:

Click on the folder icon and select the desired CAD model

Place the marker as close to the camera until the image appears and slowly move it away

PART 3:

Use the arrow keys to adjust the view of the model

Enjoy!
